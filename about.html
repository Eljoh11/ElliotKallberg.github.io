Who am I?

I am a data analyst with a strong passion for data science, specializing in uncovering insights that drive business decisions. With expertise in Python, I have developed and automated various data analysis processes, enabling efficient data cleaning, manipulation, and analysis. My skill set includes data visualization, where I use tools like Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Tableau to transform complex datasets into intuitive and actionable visual insights.
I am experienced in leveraging statistical methods and machine learning techniques to build predictive models and identify trends. By combining technical proficiency with a keen analytical mindset, I excel at translating data into meaningful stories that inform strategy and improve performance.
